Why Choose BluePath?

Powerful tools built for seamless navigation, community feedback, and smart accessibility planning.

Interactive Campus Map

Navigate a digital 2D map of ADNU's campus that helps you easily find buildings, rooms, and offices at a glance.

Real-Time Navigation

Get efficient, step-by-step directions with smart route optimization—accessible on both desktop and mobile devices.

Manual Location Input

Input your current location manually to receive reliable route guidance, even without GPS access.

Crowd Density Tracker

Know which areas are crowded in real time through community reports—helping you avoid congestion.

Search & Facility Finder

Quickly search for classrooms, offices, or services by entering a building name and room number.

Event & Notification System

Stay informed with alerts about school events, club meetings, and campus activities—right when they happen.

Emergency Assistance Info

Access emergency contacts, first-aid locations, and campus exits quickly when it matters most.

Feedback & Reporting

Report broken facilities, outdated info, or blocked areas to keep the campus data accurate and helpful for everyone.

Accessibility Highlights

See important accessibility details like ramps, elevators, restrooms, bike racks, and water stations at a glance.

Virtual Tour for Freshmen

Explore the campus online through an interactive walkthrough, perfect for new students before their first day.
