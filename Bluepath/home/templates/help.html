Help & FAQs

Everything you need to know about using BluePath

What is BluePath?
BluePath is an interactive campus navigation and information platform that helps you explore locations like Xavier Hall, Burns Hall, or any room like P217 with ease.
How do I search for a room?
Use the search bar in the Explore page. Enter the name of the location (e.g., "Arrupe Hall") or room number (e.g., "P217") and BluePath will zoom in and guide you.
Can I get directions from my location?
Yes, type your current location and your destination in the search bar, and it will generate a path and highlight it on the map.
Is BluePath available offline?
Currently, BluePath requires an internet connection to provide live map and navigation features.
How can I report incorrect location info?
You can report any map issues through the "Report" button in the sidebar of the Explore page or send feedback via the contact form.
How do I reset my password?
Go to your profile settings, click "Change Password", and follow the instructions.
How do I contact support?
Email us at support@bluepath.com or use the live chat.
How do I delete my account?
Go to Account Settings > Delete Account. This action is permanent.
